## Cold Call Prep

**Local connection angle:**
They're based in Thompson's Station — Nathaniel's neighbor. He can legitimately say he's seen their trucks in the area, or that he lives right there in Spring Hill and knows exactly the neighborhoods they serve. This is not a stretch — it's just true.

**What to genuinely compliment:**
Thirty years in business is no small thing. They've built something lasting in a competitive market and clearly earned their reputation — BBB A+, HomeAdvisor Pro, Chamber of Commerce member. That's worth acknowledging. They also show up consistently enough to have 273 posts, which shows they know social media matters even if the content hasn't taken off yet.

**Suggested opener:**
"Hey, is this Second Nature Lawn Care? Hey — it's Nathaniel, I'll keep this super quick. I'm actually based right here in Spring Hill — I see your trucks around all the time. I wanted to reach out — what would you think about me coming in and getting you a full month of social media content, completely free?"

**Levi's 4 questions — pre-answered for this prospect:**
1. How much of my time will this take? — "About 10–15 minutes — that's it. I come out to a job site or your shop, stay out of your crew's way, and everything else I handle behind the scenes."
2. What am I actually going to get? — "A full month of polished photo and video content — before and after shots, your team doing the work, the kind of stuff that shows people what a healthy lawn actually looks like."
3. How long until I get it? — [Fill in your standard turnaround]
4. What will this do for me? — "It builds trust before a customer ever picks up the phone. When they can see the real results — real lawns, your real crew — that's what gets them to call."

**Likely objection:**

**How to handle it:**
