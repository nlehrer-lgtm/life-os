## Cold Call Prep

**Local connection angle:**
You're literally down the road — Nathaniel is based in Spring Hill, Wolfe Customs is in Franklin. This is a neighbor call, not a cold outreach.

**What to genuinely compliment:**
1,023 posts is real dedication — Donny has clearly put in the work on social. The finished builds look sharp and the shop's reputation (5 stars, 89 reviews) speaks for itself. Worth acknowledging that the content looks good, and then pivoting to what's missing: growth and reach.

**Suggested opener:**
"Hey Donny, my name's Nathaniel — I'm a video producer based out of Spring Hill, and I came across Wolfe Customs while looking for shops in the area I really respect. Man, those builds you're putting out are legit. I actually wanted to reach out because I work with local businesses like yours doing content — and I think what you're building deserves to be seen by a lot more people. I'd love to offer you a free first month, no strings, just to show you what's possible."

**Levi's 4 questions — pre-answered:**
1. How much of my time will this take? — "About 10–15 minutes — that's it."
2. What am I actually going to get? — "A full month of polished photo and video content — photos, short-form video, reels, the works — ready to post."
3. How long until I get it? — Delivered within the same month after the one-hour shoot.
4. What will this do for me? — "It builds trust before a customer ever picks up the phone to call you — people see the quality of your work and they already want to work with you before you say a word."

**Likely objection:**
"We already post pretty regularly — I'm not sure we need help with that."

**How to handle it:**
"That's actually exactly why I reached out — you're clearly not afraid to put yourself out there, and that's rare. What I bring is the production layer on top of what you're already doing. Phone clips get you so far, but cinematic footage of a fresh wrap or a suspension build — that's what stops the scroll and makes someone text their buddy. Think of it as an upgrade to what you're already doing, not a replacement."
