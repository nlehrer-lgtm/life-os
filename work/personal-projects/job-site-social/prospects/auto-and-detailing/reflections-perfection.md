## Cold Call Prep

**Local connection angle:**
He's operating out of Westhaven and covers the same Spring Hill streets Nathaniel lives on. You're a potential customer as much as a potential partner — that's a natural "I've seen your work in the neighborhood" opener.

**What to genuinely compliment:**
What he's built at 16–17 is legitimately impressive — a professional website, online booking (fieldd.co), a real pricing structure, and a Google/Trustindex rating of 5 stars. Call that out specifically. Don't be vague.

**Suggested opener:**
"Hey Preston, my name's Nathaniel — I'm a neighbor over in Spring Hill and I came across your business while looking for a detailer. What you've built at your age is honestly remarkable. I do video and content production, and I work with local service businesses to help them show up online the way they deserve to — I'd love to offer you a free month of content and just show you what it looks like."

**Levi's 4 questions — pre-answered:**
1. How much of my time will this take? — "About 10–15 minutes — that's it."
2. What am I actually going to get? — "A full month of polished photo and video content — reels, posts, the whole thing."
3. How long until I get it? — Roughly 1–2 weeks after the shoot.
4. What will this do for me? — "It builds trust before a customer ever picks up the phone to call you. People book the detailer they've already seen do the work."

**Likely objection:**
"I already post my own before/afters — I think I'm good on social."

**How to handle it:**
"That's actually a great sign — you already understand the value. What I do is a layer on top of that: professional video and editing that makes those same results look ten times more credible to someone who's never heard of you. It's not instead of what you're doing, it's the upgrade."
