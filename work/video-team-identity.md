## Vision & Philosophy

**Core Mantra:** Excellence must be *who we are*, not just *what we do.*

**Our Battle is against... DRIFT**
- Distraction, comfortability, lack of vision, settling for "Good"
- "Spartan Drift" — once-great things fall when they get comfortable, rely on reputation over innovation, and stop adapting tactically

**Why Change Matters (what we identified):**
- Mediocrity is becoming acceptable
- Protocols are unclear
- No playbook — everyone is improvising
- Project outcome masks dysfunction

**How the team responded:**
- Raised the bar
- Sought clarity
- Owned our roles
- Embodied excellence
