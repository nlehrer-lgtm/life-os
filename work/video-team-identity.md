# MI Video Team Identity
*Video Team 20.30 — Discipleship Dept. | Updated April 2025*

## Vision
**"A new season of excellence, ownership, and vision."**

Excellence must be **who we are**, not just what we do.

### 2030 Goals
- 30 books
- 30 podcasts
- 300M resources distributed

## Why Change
Problems the team identified:
- Mediocrity was becoming acceptable
- Standards/protocols were unclear
- No playbook — everyone improvising
- Project outcomes were masking dysfunction

Response: raise the bar, seek clarity, unify as a team, **embody excellence — not just create it**.

## Core Belief
> Average Gear + Excellent Operations > Excellent Gear + Average Operations

Operations (people, systems, process) matter more than tools.

## Team Pillars — RECD

**R — Resilience**
> "It's not that I'm so smart, it's just that I stay with problems longer." — Einstein

**E — Excellence**
> "Excellence is never an accident. It is always the result of high intention, sincere effort, and intelligent execution." — Aristotle

**C — Communication**
> "Lack of communication leads to lack of trust, which leads to stress, resentment, and dysfunction." — Lencioni

**D — Discipline**
> "Discipline is the path to freedom." — Extreme Ownership
> "The price of excellence is discipline. The cost of mediocrity is disappointment." — William Arthur Ward
