# [[Podcast Machine]] — Project Context

## What This Is

[[The Podcast Machine]] is the central hub for all podcast production at [[Messenger International]] — strategy, operations, team structure, and show-level work. It produces audio and video podcast content that extends the ministry's reach through long-form conversation, teaching, and story.

## Current Phase

**Structure in place; content sparse.** The hub folder exists with skeleton files (`overview.md`, `team.md`, `workflows.md`), but those files are largely templates with placeholder content. Per-show folders live under `shows/` (currently empty).

Open work: see `STATUS.md`. The biggest open work item is populating the skeleton — listing actual shows, real roles, and the production workflow as it operates today.

## North Star (from `overview.md`)

- Right people in the right seats on every show
- Efficient, repeatable production processes
- Consistent quality that reflects the ministry's standard
- Always improving — document what works, fix what doesn't

## Nathaniel's Role

Director — overall production strategy and team health. As shows get spun up, each one likely gets its own subfolder under `shows/` with CONTEXT.md/STATUS.md scoped to that show.
