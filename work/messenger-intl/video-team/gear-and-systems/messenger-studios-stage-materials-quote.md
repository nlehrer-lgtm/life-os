# [[Messenger Studios]] — Permanent Stage · Materials Quote

General materials cost estimate for the modular 7" production stage that sits in front of the curved [[messenger-studios-led-wall|LED wall]] at [[Messenger Studios]]. Prices every line item from the [stage blueprint](messenger-studios-stage-blueprint.svg) (REV 0, 2026-06-05).

**Scope:** 15 modular 4'×4' bolt-together decks · 20'W × 12'D (center) · curved upstage edge · cable tunnel · removable fascia ports · downstage RGB+W DMX LED strip.

> **Bottom line: ≈ $2,500 in bare materials, ≈ $2,800–$3,000 all-in** with a build contingency. Optional safety/finish add-ons add ~$90. This is a **materials-only** estimate — labor, tools, and delivery are excluded (see [Assumptions & Exclusions](#assumptions--exclusions)).

## Swing Items (where the number can move most)

These three drive the most uncertainty — confirm before ordering:

1. **Leveler feet (60 × ~$5 = $300).** Light-duty furniture glides run ~$2 ea ($120); heavy-duty stage levelers run $6–$8 ea ($360–$480). The blueprint calls for 4 corner levelers per panel; shared corners between bolted panels could let you reduce the count.
2. **Carriage bolt sets (180 × ~$1.75 = $315).** Highest-count fastener line. Buying bolts/nuts/washers in bulk boxes can shave 20–30%.
3. **¾" CDX plywood (8 × ~$52 = $416).** Plywood pricing is volatile. Pre-priced birch or higher-grade ply for the deck would push this up; lower-grade sheathing could pull it down.

## Assumptions & Exclusions

**Estimate basis:** National big-box retail pricing (Home Depot / Lowe's / Amazon), mid-2026. All figures are **general estimates — verify current local pricing before ordering.** Quantities are pulled directly from the blueprint BOM.

**Sales tax:** Not included. [[Messenger International]] is a non-profit and likely purchases tax-exempt. If tax applies, add ~9.75% (TN) ≈ $250 on the core total.

**Not included in this quote:**
- **Labor** — this is an in-house [[Video Team]] build (materials only).
- **Tools** — table/circular saw, drills, impact driver, clamps, jigsaw for curved cuts, sanders. Assumes these are on hand or borrowed.
- **The LED wall itself** — already installed; see [[messenger-studios-led-wall]].
- **Delivery / freight** on lumber and sheet goods (most big-box stores deliver for a flat ~$60–$80 if needed).
- **Electrician** — assumes the LED strip's 24V PSU plugs into existing studio power and the DMX runs to the existing lighting board; no hardwiring/permit work assumed.
