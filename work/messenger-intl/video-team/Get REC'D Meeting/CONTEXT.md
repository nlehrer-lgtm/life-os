# Get REC'D Meeting

## What it is

The **[[Get REC'D|[[Get REC'D]] Meeting]]** is the [[Video Team]]'s weekly all-hands. It's the one meeting each week where the full team has extended time together to talk vision, walk through the calendar, and stay aligned as a team. It is the most important recurring meeting on the team's calendar.

**REC'D** stands for the team's four pillars:

- **R** — Resilience
- **E** — Excellence
- **C** — Communication
- **D** — Discipline

Each year the team revisits these pillars together and reminds itself of what it stands for. The weekly [[Get REC'D]] meeting is the rhythm that keeps the pillars alive in how the team works.

## When

- **Tuesdays at 8:00 AM** (always — [[Get REC'D]] is always at 8 AM)

## Format

The meeting has three parts, in this order:

### 1. Icebreaker / Opener (rotating team member)

A different team member leads this each week, rotating in **alphabetical order**. The person leading can bring any of the following:

- A short word (devotional / encouragement)
- Something they're reading in the Bible
- A fun game to play
- A short film or video to watch together as a team

This is their slot — they own it.

### 2. Updates (led by Lindy)

[[Lindy Wood|Lindy]] walks the team through:

- Weekly calendar
- What's happening this week
- Big-ticket items coming down the pipeline

### 3. Team-wide updates / vision (led by Nathaniel)

Nathaniel closes the meeting with:

- Team-wide news and updates from across the ministry
- Vision casting
- Encouragement and galvanizing the team
- Any changes happening around [[Messenger International]] that the team needs to know about

This is the section to lean into for galvanizing and encouraging the team.

## Standards

These are non-negotiable. They reflect the **Discipline** pillar:

1. **Be on time.** No showing up late to this meeting.
2. **No open computers.** The only person with a laptop open at any moment is the person currently speaking / sharing:
   - During the icebreaker → only the team member leading it
   - During updates → only [[Lindy Wood|Lindy]] (she shares to the screen)
   - During team-wide updates → only Nathaniel
   - Everyone else gives full attention to the speaker.

## Why it matters

[[Get REC'D]] is the only meeting each week with enough room to talk **vision and calendar together**. It anchors the team in the RECD pillars, keeps the calendar visible, and gives Nathaniel the platform to cast vision and shepherd the team. Missing it or treating it casually breaks the rhythm the whole team relies on.

## Prep workflow

Talking points for Nathaniel's Section 3 are captured continuously and compiled before the meeting:

1. **During the week:** when Nathaniel runs `/note` and the note mentions **"[[Get REC'D]]"**, a copy lands in `agendas/_talking-points.md` (the rolling list).
2. **Before the meeting** (Monday or Tuesday morning): Nathaniel runs `/get-recd-prep`. The skill compiles the rolling list into a clean line-item prep doc at `agendas/YYYY-MM-DD.md`, then clears the rolling file so the next week starts fresh.

The prep doc only covers Section 3 (Nathaniel's team-wide updates). The icebreaker rotation and [[Lindy Wood|Lindy]]'s calendar section are not prepped here.
