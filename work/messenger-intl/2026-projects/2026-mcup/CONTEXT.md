# Messenger Cup 2026 — Project Context

## What This Is
The 16th Annual Messenger Cup — Messenger International's primary annual fundraising event. Raises ~$4 million per year to fund global gospel resource distribution across 100+ nations. ~300 business leaders attend as underwriters, participants, and sponsors.

**Dates:** July 22–25, 2026
**Venue:** The Broadmoor Resort & Spa, Colorado Springs, CO
**Most important night:** Thursday, July 23 — banquet and fundraiser auction

## 2026 Theme
**"PERSEVERE — A People of Perseverance"**
Scripture anchor: Revelation 3:8 — *"I have placed before you an open door that no one can shut."*
Core message: America's prophetic mandate to spread the gospel through active, opposed perseverance. Not nationalism — mission.

## Centerpiece Deliverable: Opener Video
- **Runtime:** 1.5–2.5 minutes
- **When it plays:** Thursday night, July 23, after dinner, immediately before the fundraiser auction
- **Emotional goal:** Move the room deeply before paddles go up

**Concept:** One visual motif — a door. People from different nations press through persecution and opposition toward the same ancient wooden door. The knocking becomes music. At the climax, the door opens slightly and warm light reveals the Broadmoor ballroom — paddles on every seat.

**Final screen text:** *"I have placed before you an open door that no one can shut." — Revelation 3:8 / The nations are knocking. Tonight — we step through.*

**Reference:** `mcup-2026.md` — everything: event logistics, schedule, video team responsibilities, brand guide, Lisa's creative notes, opener concept, both scripts.
