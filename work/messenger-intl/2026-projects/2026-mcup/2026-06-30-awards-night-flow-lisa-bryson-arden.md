## The Big Decision — the honoring video is NOT the opener

It moves from the **opener** slot to the **end of the night** as the emotional/impact close. Playing a somber honoring moment cold, before the lighthearted awards (Matt & Alec joking, "Last in the Kingdom Award," etc.), would feel disrespectful and land flat with no context for Don. Everyone agreed (Arden, Lisa, Bryson, Nathaniel).

## New Order of the Friday Awards Night

1. **Opener = the RECAP video** (new this year — recap normally plays at the end). Sets the vibe, people love seeing themselves, and it primes the room since awards are based on what happened. No pressure to reveal the final number here.
2. **[[Matt]] & Alec host** the awards ceremony — all awards + all prizes.
3. **[[John Bevere|John]] & [[Lisa Bevere|Lisa]] take the stage**, say a few words, and introduce the honoring segment.
4. **[[Phil]] & [[Mary]] come up** — John & Lisa host a moment honoring **Dana** and **Don** (the [[Liberator]] / Cruz families on stage).
5. **Cue the honoring/impact video** (a reworked version of what Josh built) — now a celebration of Thursday night's impact, **ending with the final number raised**.
6. **Moment of celebration** on stage — families hug, hands up. John & Lisa **close the night in prayer.**

John will want input on the awards→honoring transition; he's unavailable right now (recovering) so we'll confirm with him later.
