# Video Team — Mission (General Audience)

_The plain-English version. No insider jargon — for anyone who doesn't know what the 3 C's are. Use this when introducing the team to a new partner, a vendor, a board member, or anyone outside the [[Discipleship Team]]. The insider version with 3 C's framing lives in [[Role and Mission|role-and-mission.md]]._

---

## Who we are

The **[[Video Team]]** at [[Messenger International]] is the team that turns the ministry's vision into footage that honors what makes it powerful.

When the leaders of [[Messenger International|Messenger]] have a teaching, a story, or a moment they want the world to receive, we are the team that translates it into something people can actually watch — without losing the weight of what was said.

---

## Who we serve

Three audiences, in order:

1. **The global viewer.** The person, somewhere in the world, who is going to encounter [[Messenger International]] through a screen. Especially the one who'd never otherwise hear from [[John Bevere|John]] and Lisa. They are the ultimate aim. Everything we shoot points to them.
2. **The [[John Bevere|Beveres]] and ministry leadership.** [[John Bevere|John]], Lisa, [[Arden]]. Our daily job is translating their voice and vision into something that scales beyond their physical presence — we are the multiplier on their reach.
3. **Our partners across the ministry.** The teams that plan the content and shape the message. Our job is to make sure their work can actually be seen, heard, and shared.

---

## What we uniquely own

**The craft of how [[Messenger International|Messenger]]'s content looks and sounds.**

- Cinematography
- Editing
- Sound design
- Color
- Deliverable quality
- The aesthetic and technical standard the ministry's content gets held to

The standard our content meets — visually, sonically, emotionally — is on us. When [[Messenger International]] sends out a video, the production quality has to match the weight of what's being said. If the craft is weak, the message is undercut. Keeping that from happening is our job.

---

## The Director's role (Nathaniel)

Two things:

1. **Keep the craft at a level the ministry can stake its message on.**
2. **Develop the team so the craft compounds over time.** [[Lindy Wood|Lindy]], [[Andrew McIntosh|Andrew]], [[Josh Kendrick|Josh]], [[Annie Duda|Annie]], [[LeVann Anthony Jr|LeVann]] — they execute the work. The standard gets sharper every year through them, not through hiring.
