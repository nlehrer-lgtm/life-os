# Discipleship Team — Project Context

## What This Is

The **Discipleship Team** at Messenger International is the cross-functional group responsible for producing Messenger's discipleship content — the videos, outlines, and packaged resources that disciple people globally. It is anchored by a weekly Monday morning **Touch Base Huddle**.

The team uses the **3 C's** as its operating model:

- **Coordinate** — Arden & Adrian — direction, scheduling, alignment with the Beveres
- **Create** — Chris Pace (Content) — packaging, outlines, plans for what gets shot
- **Capture** — Nathaniel & Lindy (Video Team) — capturing the content on set, post

## Touch Base Huddle

- **When:** Mondays, 11:00 – 11:30 AM
- **Organizer:** Arden Bevere
- **Attendees:** Arden Bevere, Adrian Livingston, Chris Pace, Lindy Wood, Nathaniel Lehrer

**Purpose:** Touch base across the 3 C's. Each lead comes with the high-level items from their lane that are pertinent for the rest of the team. Not a status meeting on everything — a curated share of what matters for the others to know.

**Original framing from Arden's invite:**
> "Use this meeting to acknowledge the vital roles each of you plays in the ability of Messenger to disciple people effectively. Starting our week with a clear understanding of each other's plans will enhance our collaboration and ensure we work together seamlessly... I want to make sure that these teams never become their own silos."

## Nathaniel's Role in This Meeting

Nathaniel represents **Capture** — the Video Team's lane. He comes with:
- What the video team is currently shooting / in post on
- Upcoming captures the rest of the team should be aware of
- Constraints, blockers, or asks for Coordinate/Create
- Reminders of what the video team's role actually is (per today's inbox note — there's an ongoing need to reinforce role clarity)

## Prep Workflow

Run `/discipleship-huddle-prep` Monday morning before the 11:00 AM huddle. The skill pulls:

1. Most recent Granola transcript titled "Discipleship Team Touch Base Huddle" (if any)
2. Recent `notes/inbox.md` entries mentioning "discipleship"
3. Active work project STATUS.md / CONTEXT.md files for what's currently in motion
4. Today's relevant `work/messenger-intl/**` activity

Compiles into a one-page prep doc at `prep-YYYY-MM-DD.md` (Monday's date), framed by the 3 C's.
