## Fit Assessment

**Score: 7/10**

Touchstone is a strong service-business fit. Maurice has built a real operation with proven quality and loyal customers, but his social media doesn't reflect that — 251 Instagram followers after 13+ years in business is a significant gap between reputation and online presence. There's no owner on camera, no video that builds trust before a call. The content exists but it doesn't work hard enough.

Geography is the one flag: they're Nashville-based at their shop, not Franklin/Spring Hill. However, they have dedicated service pages for Franklin and Brentwood, and reviews suggest they regularly work in those areas. This earns partial geography credit — they actively serve your target market even if they don't live in it. Deduct 1 point for not being locally rooted in the Franklin/Spring Hill corridor.

## Cold Call Prep

**Local connection angle:**
Touchstone has a specific "Franklin, TN" service page and reviews from Franklin/Brentwood customers. You're based in Spring Hill — you can genuinely say you're a neighbor. "I'm actually in Spring Hill, right down 65 from you — I've seen your work come up when I'm searching for detailers down here."

**What to genuinely compliment:**
141 five-star reviews and 13 years in business is remarkable. That's a brand built entirely on word-of-mouth and quality — Maurice clearly does incredible work. Lead with that honestly.

**Suggested opener:**
"Hey, is this Maurice? My name's Nathaniel — I'm actually based in Spring Hill, right down 65 from the Franklin area where your guys work. I came across Touchstone while doing some research on local detailing businesses and honestly, 141 five-star reviews after 13 years — that's legit. I wanted to reach out because I run a content service specifically for service businesses like yours, and I think you're leaving a lot of trust on the table online. Your work is incredible but your Instagram doesn't show it yet."

**Levi's 4 questions — pre-answered:**
1. How much of my time will this take? — "About 10–15 minutes — that's it."
2. What am I actually going to get? — "A full month of polished photo and video content."
3. How long until I get it? — Delivered within 1 week of filming.
4. What will this do for me? — "It builds trust before a customer ever picks up the phone to call you — so when they find you on Instagram or Google, they're already sold."

**Likely objection:**
"We already post on Instagram and it's fine — I'm busy enough through referrals."

**How to handle it:**
"That makes sense — referrals are the best customers, and you've clearly built that well. This isn't a replacement for that; think of it as a way to make every referral easier. When someone gets your name from a friend and goes to look you up, your Instagram becomes a second handshake. Right now it's not doing that work for you — and the first month is on me, so there's nothing to lose in seeing what it could look like."
