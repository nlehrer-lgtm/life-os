**Estimated annual revenue:** $500K–$1M

With 107 Google reviews at 5.0 stars and at least two locations (Franklin and East Nashville), they're clearly established. However, auto detailing — even premium — has a ceiling without a large fleet or heavy commercial volume. Multiple locations and dealer relationships push them toward the upper end of this range.

## Fit Assessment

**Score: 6/10**

Corsair is a blue-collar, service-based business in the heart of Williamson County — that's +5 right there. But they lose points on the social media weakness front: they already post reels and have some video presence, which reduces the urgency of the pitch. They're not at -4 territory (their content lacks real personality and faces), but it's not a clean "you have zero video" conversation either. The best angle here is the gap between their polished car shots and the human story behind the shop — Janis, the techs, the culture. That's where the pitch lives.

## Cold Call Prep

**Local connection angle:**
They're the official detailer for Nashville Cars & Coffee — Middle Tennessee's biggest car enthusiast meetup. That's a hyperlocal prestige signal. Mention you've seen the brand around the Franklin/Brentwood car scene and wanted to reach out.

**What to genuinely compliment:**
Their quality of work is clearly exceptional — 107 reviews at 5.0 stars on ceramic coatings and PPF is rare. The dealer partnerships are a real credibility marker. Compliment the brand reputation, not just the car photos.

**Suggested opener:**
"Hey, is this Janis? This is Nathaniel — I'm a local content creator based in Spring Hill, and I've seen Corsair's work around the Cars & Coffee crowd. I wanted to reach out because I think your shop has a story that your Instagram photos honestly don't tell yet — and I'd love to show you what that could look like, on us, for the first month."

**Levi's 4 questions — pre-answered:**
1. How much of my time will this take? — "About 10–15 minutes — that's it."
2. What am I actually going to get? — "A full month of polished photo and video content — reels, stories, the kind of stuff that makes people feel like they already trust you before they call."
3. How long until I get it? — Typically delivered within the same month, ready to post on a schedule.
4. What will this do for me? — "It builds trust before a customer ever picks up the phone to call you. People book the shop they feel like they know — not just the one with the nicest car photos."

**Likely objection:**
"We already do our own videos and we get good engagement."

**How to handle it:**
"That's great — and it shows. What I'd add on top of that is the kind of content that introduces people to the team and the culture. Customers who already know your face and trust your story don't shop around. Think of it as a layer on what you're already doing — not a replacement."
