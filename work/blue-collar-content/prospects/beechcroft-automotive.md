## Cold Call Prep

**Local connection angle:**
Nathaniel lives in Spring Hill and drives by the shop on Main Street. David is a well-known local name — his story ran in Spring Hill Local. This isn't a cold call from a stranger; it's a neighbor reaching out. The brother connection (Spring Hill Automotive, 621 Beechcroft Rd) is also local color worth knowing — David has deep roots here.

**What to genuinely compliment:**
His reputation is real and hard-earned. 4.9 stars, people in reviews calling him honest, family-oriented, community-minded. That's actually the heart of the pitch — he's already earned the trust, he just isn't showing it to anyone who doesn't already know him.

**Suggested opener:**
"Hey David, it's Nathaniel — I'll keep this super quick. I actually live right here in Spring Hill, so I drive by you guys all the time on Main Street. I was looking at your Facebook page — you guys clearly do great work, your customers love you. I wanted to reach out — what would you think about me coming in and getting you a full month of social media content, completely free?"

**Levi's 4 questions — pre-answered for this prospect:**
1. How much of my time will this take? — "About 10–15 minutes — that's it. I stay out of your way the whole time."
2. What am I actually going to get? — "A full month of polished photo and video content, ready to post — your shop, your team, your work, your voice."
3. How long until I get it? — [Fill in Nathaniel's standard turnaround — typically ~1 week after shoot]
4. What will this do for me? — "It puts your reputation on video. People who don't know you yet get to see exactly what your regulars already know — that you're the guy in Spring Hill who tells them the truth."

**Likely objection:**
"We already get most of our business from word-of-mouth — we don't really need social media."

**How to handle it:**
"That's honestly the best kind of business, and it tells me everything I need to know about how you operate. All I'm doing is putting that same word-of-mouth on video — so the person who's new to Spring Hill, or whose neighbor just recommended you, can go look you up and immediately see exactly what your regulars already know. You've already done the hard part. I'm just making sure the right people can find it."
