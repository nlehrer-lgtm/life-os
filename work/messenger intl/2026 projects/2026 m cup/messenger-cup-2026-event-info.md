## Overview

The 16th Annual Messenger Cup — an annual golf tournament & alpine experience hosted by Messenger International (founded by John and Lisa Bevere). Brings together approximately 250 leaders in business, church, and the arts for an intimate, relationship-focused experience.

**Mission:** All proceeds support Messenger International's global outreach — distributing discipleship resources in multiple languages to leaders across 100+ nations.

## About Messenger International

- Founded: July 8, 1980 (prophetic vision by John Bevere)
- By 2021: Distributed 41+ million resources to leaders in 100+ nations in 120+ languages
- Digital platform: MessengerX — enables one-on-one and group discipleship via phone, tablet, or computer
- Philosophy: Based on Luke 14:12–14 — provide resources to those who cannot repay
