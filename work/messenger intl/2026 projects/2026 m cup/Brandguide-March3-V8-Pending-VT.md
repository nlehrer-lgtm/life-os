## Typography

| Font | Use | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Tiempos Headline Light | Headlines, serif editorial text | All caps, kerning ~160; use italics for emphasis in body |
| ABC Chanel | Title text | All caps, kerning ~140, +0.25 stroke, bold feel |
| Founders Grotesk | Subheads, all weights, title or all caps | Kerning variable (~60) |
| Founders Grotesk Mono | Micro text | Kerning 80–200 range |
| Altesse | Script accent | Title case |
