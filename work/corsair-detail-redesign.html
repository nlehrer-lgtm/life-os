About Corsair

Corsair Detail is a professional automotive protection studio serving the Greater Nashville area. We bring meticulous attention to detail and premium-grade products to every vehicle that comes through our doors.

By appointment only — because every vehicle deserves our full, undivided attention. We recommend professional detailing every 4–6 months to preserve your vehicle's finish and protect your investment.

What We Do

Our Services

01 — Detailing

Auto Detailing

Comprehensive interior and exterior treatment — washing, polishing, waxing, deep carpet and upholstery cleaning, and engine bay detailing. Typically 4–5 hours.

Interior & Exterior
02 — Protection

Ceramic Coatings

A nano-ceramic protective layer that bonds to your paint — delivering scratch resistance, UV protection, and a hydrophobic self-cleaning finish with enhanced gloss.

Long-Term Protection
03 — Film

Paint Protection Film

Transparent, self-healing urethane film that guards against rock chips, road debris, and scratches. Customizable coverage from partial front-end to full wrap.

Self-Healing PPF
04 — Tint

Ceramic Window Tint

Premium ceramic tint that blocks heat, glare, and UV radiation — without signal interference. Enhanced privacy and interior comfort for daily driving.

Heat & UV Block
05 — Eco Clean

Ice Blasting

Eco-friendly, non-abrasive dry ice blasting that removes grime, grease, and residue from hard-to-reach areas without chemicals or water damage.

Eco-Friendly
06 — Commercial

Commercial & Residential Tint

Professional window film installation for homes and commercial spaces — reducing energy costs, blocking UV, and enhancing privacy and aesthetics.
