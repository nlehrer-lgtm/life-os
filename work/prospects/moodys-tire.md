## Cold Call Notes

**Hook:** 82-year-old family business with a great story — three generations, founded by two brothers, one went to war and one kept the shop alive. That story sells itself. They just aren't telling it on video.

**Pain point:** 548 Instagram posts with no professional video production. 4,600+ Facebook likes with no video content presence. Their biggest asset is their story and their people, and none of it is on camera.

**Angle:** "You've got one of the best brand stories in Franklin — 82 years, three generations, started by two brothers during World War II. That story should be on video. Your competitors don't have that."

**Decision maker to ask for:** Jimmy Moody (Co-Owner, LinkedIn confirmed) or James M. Moody, Jr. (President)

**Warmth signals:** Faith and family are explicit core values. They use language like "neighbors" and "family." Warm, trust-first culture — match that tone on the call.

**Objection to prep for:** "We've been around 82 years without it." Response: "And you've built something incredible — this is about not leaving that story on the table when your competitors in Brentwood and Spring Hill are investing in video."
