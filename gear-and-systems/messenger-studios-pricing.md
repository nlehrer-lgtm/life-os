# [[Messenger Studios]] — Rate Card

For external booking inquiries. Studio operated by the [[Video Team]] at [[Messenger International]]. All bookings subject to ministry-schedule approval; ministry productions take priority.

**Source:** `gear-and-systems/source-documents/Messenger Studio Pricing Sheet.pdf`

---

## Studio Overview

Boutique virtual-production studio for high-quality content: podcasts, commercials, promo videos, short-form cinematic.

**Key capabilities:**
- 1.9mm [[Planar]] LED wall with [[Brompton]] processing
- [[Unreal Engine]] virtual production
- Professional lighting grid: Aputure P600, P300, Infinibars + full grip
- Broadcast-quality audio for multi-person productions
- Flexible layout — seated, standing, cinematic

Full gear and signal-flow reference: [[messenger-studios-overview]].

---

## Facility Rates

| Tier | Duration | Rate |
|---|---|---|
| **Half Day** | Up to 5 hours | **$2,000** |
| **Full Day** | Up to 9 hours | **$3,500** |

Both tiers include:
- Studio access
- LED wall powered, calibrated, ready
- Basic signal routing to wall (plate playback or feed)
- Full lighting grid + grip package
- Audio routing and monitoring
- Standard power and infrastructure

## Required Labor — LED Wall / Virtual Production Shoots

These roles are required for any LED-wall production to ensure quality, safety, and proper operation.

### Virtual Production / Unreal Supervisor

| Tier | Rate |
|---|---|
| Half Day | $450 |
| Full Day | $800 |

Responsible for:
- [[Unreal Engine]] operation
- LED wall content management and playback
- Scene loading and troubleshooting
- Tracking oversight (if applicable)
- Wall calibration and technical integrity

### Studio Supervisor / Production Lead

| Tier | Rate |
|---|---|
| Half Day | $400 |
| Full Day | $700 |

Responsible for:
- Client interface and on-set leadership
- Studio operations and workflow oversight
- Enforcement of studio policies and safeguards
- Coordination of video / audio / lighting systems
- General production support
