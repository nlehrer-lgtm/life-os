# Food

The hub for everything food in the Lehrer house — built to make food decisions easier. When the question is *where do we eat, what do we eat, or what do we need to buy*, the answer should live here.

## Why this exists

There's a lot of daily decision-making around food. This folder collects the recipes, meals, lists, and go-to spots worth remembering so the answer is already written down instead of re-decided every time. Simple over neat — add things as they come up.

## What lives here

- **Recipes** — recipes worth keeping and making again
- **Family meals** — our favorite meals on rotation (and what [[Ellie]] makes)
- **Snacks** — favorite snacks we buy and want to restock
- **Grocery lists** — go-to Costco and Publix runs
- **Store deals** — deals we like to grab and where
- **Restaurants** — places we genuinely love, for when we want to eat out

## How to use it

- **"What should we make this week?"** → pull from family meals + recipes
- **"What do we need at Costco/Publix?"** → grocery lists
- **"Where do we want to eat?"** → restaurants
- **"What snacks are we out of?"** → snacks list

## Quick capture

When a new recipe, meal, snack, deal, or restaurant comes up, drop it in here — don't overthink the structure. A loose note now beats a perfect file never. New file per topic when it grows; until then, capture is the win.
