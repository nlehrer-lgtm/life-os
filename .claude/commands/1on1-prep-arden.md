## Philosophy
Per the *Developing Messengers — The Weekly 1:1* guide (`work/messenger-intl/leadership/developing-messengers-weekly-1on1.pdf`):

> **The team member owns and drives the agenda.** They bring priorities, progress, challenges, and feedback.
>
> **The leader sets the context.** Team and department objectives, vision and direction, current priorities, role alignment.
>
> *"The goal is development, not direction. Ask more, tell less."*

In this 1:1, **Nathaniel is the team member.** Arden is the leader. This prep doc gives Nathaniel the agenda HE will bring to the meeting plus strategic questions HE wants Arden's input on.

**This is the deep prep — not the day-of glance sheet.** That's `/1on1-final-arden`. The expected loop: run this skill → read the prep → edit/adjust it → run `/1on1-final-arden` for the glance sheet → walk into the meeting → run `/1on1-debrief-arden` after.
